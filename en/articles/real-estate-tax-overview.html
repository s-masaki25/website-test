Real Estate Tax Overview

Cross-border real estate transactions involve complex tax considerations that require careful analysis and planning.

This article provides an overview of key tax issues for individuals and entities involved in international real estate investments.

Types of Real Estate Taxes

Real estate taxation encompasses various levies including acquisition taxes, holding taxes, and capital gains taxes upon disposition. Each jurisdiction applies different rules and rates.

Cross-Border Considerations

International property transactions may trigger tax obligations in multiple jurisdictions. Understanding applicable tax treaties and planning strategies is essential to optimize outcomes.
